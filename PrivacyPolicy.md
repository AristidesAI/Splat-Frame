# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-02-25

This Privacy Policy describes how **Splat-Frame** (“the app”) handles information.

## Summary
- The app **does not collect, store, or share** personal data.
- The app processes content **only on your device**.
- The app does not sell data and does not use data for advertising.

## Information We Collect
**None.**  
We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, identifiers, or location data.

## On-Device Processing
Any content you provide to the app (for example, images, video, text, or other files) is processed locally on your device. That content is not transmitted to us.

## Data Sharing
We do not share your data with third parties because we do not collect it.

## Data Retention
We do not retain your data on our servers. Any data the app creates (such as settings or output files) remains on your device unless you delete it.

## Third-Party Services
The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.  
If you access third-party links or services from within the app (if any), those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

## Children’s Privacy
The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children, and it does not collect personal information from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy
If the app’s behavior changes in a way that affects privacy, we will update this Privacy Policy and change the “Last updated” date above.
