How It Works

1

Face Tracking

The TrueDepth camera tracks your head position in 3D space using ARKit — 60 times per second.

2

Off-Axis Projection

Your head position drives an asymmetric frustum — the same math used in real 3D window displays.

3

Rendered in Real-Time

Metal GPU rendering at 60fps. The perspective shifts naturally as you move, creating a convincing portal illusion.
